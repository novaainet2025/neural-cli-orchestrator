# Nova Government Dispute Resolution Policy (Discussion 10 & 11)

## 1. Jurisdiction by Dispute Type (opencode)
- **Economic Disputes (Escrow)**: Handled by the **Economic Arbitration Authority (EAA)**. Procedure includes escrow fund verification, automated mediation triggers, and settlement execution via the Escrow API.
- **Domain Squatting**: Managed by the **Digital Identity Office (DIO)**. Process involves domain ownership verification and remediation actions.
- **Copyright Infringement**: Overseen by the **Intellectual Property Commission (IPC)**. Follows the **3-Stage Cultural Dispute Process** (Section 6).
- **Governance Appeals**: Processed by the **Governance Review Board (GRB)**. Handles policy interpretation and procedural challenges.

## 2. Mediator Panel Composition (gemini)
- **Verified Citizens**: Only users with a `verified` badge may serve as mediators.
- **Selection Criteria**: Reputation > 80, no conflict‑of‑interest flags, and at least 1 year of verified activity.
- **Term Length**: 12 months, renewable once.
- **Recusal Procedure**: Mediators may file a recusal request via the `POST /mediator/recuse` endpoint; conflicts are reviewed by the **Conflict‑Resolution Committee**.

## 3. Automated Escrow Dispute Workflow (codex)
- **Trigger**: Dispute creation (`POST /api/v1/escrow/dispute`) automatically starts a 48‑hour mediation timer.
- **Timeline**:
  1. **0‑48 h** – Automatic notification to parties and mediator panel.
  2. **48‑96 h** – If no settlement, AI‑mediator proposes a resolution.
  3. **96‑120 h** – Final decision executed; escrow funds released or returned.

## 4. Appeals Process (opencode)
| Stage | Body | Duration | Cost (USD) |
|-------|------|----------|------------|
| 1st Instance | Mediator Panel | 14 days | $0 (state‑funded) |
| 2nd Instance | Governance Review Board | 30 days | $200 administrative fee |
| Final | Constitutional Vote (citizen‑wide) | 60 days | $500 voting logistics |

## 5. Enforcement & Rewards (gemini)
- **Sanctions**: Automated account freeze, reputation penalty, and optional black‑list entry for non‑compliance.
- **Blacklist Integration**: Non‑paying parties are added to the **Public Non‑Compliant Registry** after a 7‑day grace period.
- **Winner Compensation**: Successful parties receive a reward token (`RWD`) transferred via the **Reward Service** (`POST /rewards/issue`).

## 6. Copyright Infringement: 3-Stage Process (gemini/opencode)
Copyright disputes follow a specialized escalation path to ensure cultural integrity:

1.  **Stage 1: Peer Adjustment (72h)**: Initial mediation between parties facilitated by one assigned peer mediator.
2.  **Stage 2: Governance Committee Review**: If no consensus is reached, the dispute is escalated to a 5-member Committee of verified citizens.
3.  **Stage 3: Multi-sig Mediation (3/5)**: Final binding adjudication executed via a 3/5 multi-signature agreement.

**Penalties for Infringement**:
- Mandatory recovery of all profits generated by the infringing asset.
- **200% Punitive Damages** based on the total profit amount.
- Permanent blacklist from Nova Library for the infringing DID.
